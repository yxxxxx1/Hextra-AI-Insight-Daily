These little guys let the Company count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking out how popular a certain section is and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.
